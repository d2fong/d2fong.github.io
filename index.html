Take a look at some of my recent work! My main interest is in the visual aspects of computing. Human Computer Interaction and User Interfaces were what captured my attention the most during my studies. Here is a collection of projects that I have done - from personal projects to work I have done at various companies like Minted and Autodesk's 3d printing platform - Spark

Contextual Design - Imprint

In a team of 4, we studied the university newspaper in an effort to understand their work domain, find task breakdowns and pain points, and prototype solutions that address these problems. This project is a product of four months of user interviews, work modelling, prototyping, and analysis that aimed to help our users perform better.

Print Studio v1.5 UI

During my co-op term at Autodesk, I worked with the Spark Platform team to create a UI that allows users to send, monitor, and control print jobs to local 3d printers. Built with Node, Angular, Bootstrap, and SASS.
